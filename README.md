# 🎮 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis for an Online Gaming Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive end-to-end Product Analytics case study for an online gaming platform using **BigQuery SQL, Python, and Power BI**. The objective is to analyze the complete player lifecycle—from customer acquisition to long-term retention, customer lifetime value, and executive business performance.

The analysis follows the entire customer journey:

**Registration → First Deposit → First Bet → Active Player → Retention → Revenue → Customer Lifetime Value**

Using transactional gaming data, this project identifies conversion bottlenecks, customer behavior patterns, high-value player segments, marketing effectiveness, promotional ROI, cohort performance, and overall business profitability.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The project aims to answer key business questions such as:

- Where do players drop off during the customer journey?
- Which acquisition channels generate the highest-value customers?
- What factors drive long-term customer retention?
- Which products contribute the highest revenue and profitability?
- How effective are bonus campaigns?
- Which customers generate the highest lifetime value?
- How can marketing spend be optimized?
- What KPIs should executives monitor for business growth?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## Customer Acquisition

- Acquired **292,785 registered players**
- Customer acquisition heavily concentrated in **Affiliate, PPC, and Unknown** channels
- PPC generated the highest-value customers despite fewer registrations

---

## Conversion Funnel

- Registration → Deposit Conversion: **43.47%**
- Registration → First Bet (30 Days): **49.75%**
- Largest customer drop-off occurs before the first deposit

---

## Customer Engagement

- **167,327 active players**
- Most customers remain active for only **1–5 days**
- Longer engagement directly increases customer value and revenue

---

## Revenue Analysis

- **₹2.90 Billion** Total Bets
- **₹111.04 Million** Net Gaming Revenue
- **ARPU: ₹663.61**
- **House Edge: 3.83%**

---

## Product Analytics

- eGaming generated **61%** of total revenue
- SportsBook delivered significantly higher profit margins
- Non-Table Games were the highest-performing sub-product

---

## Customer Segmentation

- VIP players generated the majority of business revenue
- Customer value is highly concentrated among top spenders
- 35–44 age group generated the highest lifetime value

---

## Cohort Analysis

- Early customer churn is highest within the first month
- January 2020 cohort generated the highest lifetime revenue
- October–November 2020 cohorts showed the strongest engagement

---

## Promotional Analysis

- Bonus recipients demonstrated significantly higher engagement
- eGaming promotions produced the strongest promotional ROI
- Promotional effectiveness varies across customer segments
